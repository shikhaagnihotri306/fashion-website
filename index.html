INDIAN MAKEUP BRANDS AND THEIR OWNER...

MY GLAM BY MANISH MALHOTRA

Manish Malhotra is one of the most popular and successful fashion designers in India. He also happens to have one of the best celebrity makeup lines in the country. Manish has worked closely with almost every Bollywood star and his creations are loved by all. His designs are the definition of couture and his latest beauty venture with MyGlamm has the word ‘luxe’ labelled all over it. Built on the philosophy of celebrating glamour, Manish Malhotra’s haute couture makeup line is designed to make a woman feel glamorous and gorgeous in her skin. From the sleek black and rose gold packaging, to the quality of each product from the makeup line – everything is a 10/10


KAY BEAUTY BY KATRINA KAIF

Most recently Katrina Kaif came up with her beauty line called Kay Beauty. The actress herself launched the brand in November 2019 in Mumbai. Even before it was made available to consumers, the products made headlines as a lot of stars indulged in promoting Katrina’s new venture. Speaking about Kay Beauty to one of the top media houses, Katrina Kaif said: “Makeup has been a huge part of my journey; right from a model, to an actor to magazine covers to countless appearances. “It has always been an interest of mine and something I’ve been passionate about.

LAKME ABSOLUTE BY KAREENA KAPOOR KHAN

After making a name for herself in the Bollywood industry, Kareena Kapoor Khan then got into the beauty business. In 2018, the actress launched her makeup line called Kareena Kapoor Khan by Lakmé Absolute. It’s her first-ever makeup line which features products like cheek contours, face contours, brow definers, lipsticks, mascara and more. The brand’s philosophy revolves around channelling every woman’s inner diva. She is also the brand ambassador for Lakmé and has immense love for the brand as well. As far as the look and feel of the products are concerned, each of the products come in attractive black and gold packaging.
